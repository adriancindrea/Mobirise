Refund policy:

Eligibility for refunds

To be eligible for a refund, the ticket must not be older than a month. ( for example: you bought it on the 1st of june => you cannot get a refund after the 1st of july )

Refund process

If you wish to return a ticket, scan the QR code you received in the mail after purchasing the ticket. After that, you will see the following options: check-in, transfer and refund. Press "Refund", then confirm your action by stating the name on the card used to buy the ticket. The money will be refunded to that card in 2-4 bussines days.
