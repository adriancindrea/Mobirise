DAY 0
The festival will be open, but no live performance will take place. This is a great opportunity to explore the grounds, take pics, have fun and enjoy the attractions without having to worry about missing your favourite artist.

DAY 1
The first artist of each stage will perform on this day. They will all perform for an hour, in order of the stage layout above. Don't worry about missing the start of your favourite artist. In between each act, there is a 30 minute break so you can peacefully reach the next stage.
